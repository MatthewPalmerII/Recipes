Chicken Parmesean

Description

This chicken parmesean is nothing like other dinners you have had. My chicken parmesean is not breaded to make it healthier for you to eat. This chicken pairs great with a side of spaghetti and some brusselsprouts.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breast
  • 4 Slices of Mozzarella cheese
  • 1ish Tablespoons of Italian Seasonings
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

    • Steps

    1. Cut in half, or butterfly the chicken breasts.
    2. Brush lightly with oil.
    3. Sprinkle with the italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
    4. Heat a pan (preferably cast iron) with 2 tablespoons of oil.
    5. Cook chicken on each side for about 4-5 minutes depending on size. Chicken should reach an internal temperature of 165 degrees Fahrenheit.
    6. About a minute before the chicken has finished, place the cheese slices on top of the chicken.
    7. Once cheese has melted, turn off burner and add the sauce to the pan.
    8. Preheat broiler to high heat.
    9. Stick the entire pan into the broiler. Leave it in the broiler until the cheese has browned to your liking.
    10. Serve Immediately with appropriate sides.
